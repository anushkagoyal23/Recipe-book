Pasta Dishes

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • 50g Parmesan
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions:

1. Cook the spaghetti.
2. Fry the pancetta.
3. Beat the eggs and mix with cheese.
4. Combine everything and serve.

Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 200g Fettuccine
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parsley

Instructions:

1. Cook the fettuccine.
2. Cook the chicken.
3. Prepare the sauce.
4. Combine everything and serve.

Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 200g Pasta
  • 1 cup fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

1. Cook the pasta.
2. Blend basil, Parmesan, pine nuts, and garlic.
3. Add olive oil slowly.
4. Mix pesto with pasta and serve.

Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan

Instructions:

1. Cook lasagna noodles.
2. Cook beef, onion, and garlic.
3. Layer noodles, beef, ricotta, mozzarella, and sauce.
4. Bake and serve.

Mac and Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 200g macaroni
  • 2 cups cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

1. Cook the macaroni.
2. Make the cheese sauce with butter, flour, milk, and cheese.
3. Combine macaroni with sauce and serve.

Ravioli

Ingredients:

  • 200g ravioli
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Basil for garnish

Instructions:

1. Cook the ravioli.
2. Prepare the filling with ricotta, egg, and Parmesan.
3. Serve ravioli with marinara sauce and garnish with basil.

Linguine with Clam Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 200g linguine
  • 500g clams
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Parsley for garnish
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

1. Cook the linguine.
2. Cook garlic in olive oil.
3. Add clams and white wine.
4. Toss with linguine and garnish with parsley.

Orzo Salad

Ingredients:

  • 200g orzo
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup olives
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

1. Cook the orzo.
2. Chop vegetables.
3. Mix orzo with vegetables, feta, and olives.
4. Dress with olive oil and lemon juice.

Tortellini Soup

Ingredients:

  • 200g tortellini
  • 1 liter chicken broth
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 celery stalks
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup spinach
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

1. Cook vegetables in broth.
2. Add tortellini and cook until tender.
3. Stir in spinach and season to taste.

Baked Ziti

Ingredients:

  • 200g ziti
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

1. Cook the ziti.
2. Brown the beef and mix with marinara.
3. Layer ziti, ricotta, beef sauce, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
4. Bake and serve.

Rotini Primavera

Ingredients:

  • 200g rotini
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Basil for garnish

Instructions:

1. Cook the rotini.
2. Sauté vegetables in olive oil.
3. Toss with rotini and Parmesan.
4. Garnish with basil and serve.

Cannelloni

Ingredients:

  • 12 cannelloni tubes
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parsley for garnish

Instructions:

1. Cook beef, onion, and garlic.
2. Stuff cannelloni tubes with beef and ricotta mixture.
3. Arrange in a baking dish, top with marinara sauce and Parmesan.
4. Bake and serve.
